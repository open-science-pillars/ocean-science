# NAO index: station-based vs EOF-based families

Two inequivalent families define the North Atlantic Oscillation:

- **Station-based:** Azores (or Lisbon) minus Iceland sea level
  pressure. Long records, fixed points, noisier.
- **EOF-based:** the leading EOF of North Atlantic SLP; the pattern
  adapts to the data and the era used to train it.

The two families diverge in their trend and variance behavior, so a
station NAO and an EOF NAO are not interchangeable series. The sign
convention (positive = strengthened meridional pressure gradient) must
be stated explicitly, because EOF signs are arbitrary until fixed.
